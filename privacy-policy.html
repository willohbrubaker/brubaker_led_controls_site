BrubakerLeds Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

BrubakerLeds is a simple iOS application designed exclusively for personal use to control LED strip patterns and effects in your home. It communicates directly with your private home server via local network API calls.

We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data from you or your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

This app is intentionally designed with privacy in mind and collects no personal information whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect or have access to:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers (UDID, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Location data (precise or coarse)
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or media access
  • Usage data, diagnostics, crash logs, or analytics
  • Any information about your LED hardware beyond what is necessary for real-time control commands

All communication occurs only between your iOS device and your own private home server on your local network. No data is sent to us (the developer), third-party servers, cloud services, or analytics providers.

2. How the App Works (Technical Overview)

The app sends simple HTTP/HTTPS requests (e.g., to trigger patterns, change colors, or adjust brightness) to the IP address or hostname of your home server that you configure within the app. These requests:

  • Are made only when you interact with the buttons/controls
  • Contain only the command payload you initiate (no identifiers tied to you or your device)
  • Are not logged, stored, or retained by the app after the request completes
  • Remain entirely within your private local network (unless you explicitly configure the server to be internet-accessible, which is outside the app's scope)

3. Local Network Usage

The app requires Local Network permission (iOS prompt) to discover or communicate with your home server. This permission is used solely for sending control commands and is not used to scan, track, or collect information about other devices on your network.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect app changes. Any updates will be posted here with a revised effective date. Because the app collects no data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
