BrubakerLeds Support

BrubakerLeds is a simple app made to control patterns and effects on our property's LED strips via our private server.

It communicates directly with your local home server—no cloud, no accounts, no data collection.

Common Questions & Troubleshooting

LEDs not responding or patterns not changing? Wait a moment and try again! If it still is not working, check that you are connected to the wifi (not necessary, but preferred for speed/reliability), and try again!
Local Network permission prompt? The app needs Local Network access (iOS privacy setting) to send commands to your home server. Allow it when prompted—it's only used for direct communication on your Wi-Fi.
App crashing or freezing? Try force-quitting and relaunching the app. Make sure your iOS is up to date. If the issue persists, send us details (iOS version, what you were doing).
